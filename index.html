About Us

T&D Contruction PVT LTD

T & D Construction PVT LTD is a result of a dream between two brothers, who have gained the knowledge and experience in the construction industry over a few years. These brothers began their construction journey through observing the building of their parents lodge from scratch from 2003 till present. They shadowed and learnt the art of the construction trade taking their experiences further and further. Today they stand tall with a vision to share their experiences with the word.

Our Goal

  • To provide a high-quality service at a minimal price
  • To build unique and over lasting structures
  • Building a strong trust worthy relationship with our clients

Our Vision

To work with the government in providing low cost housing to the communities.
